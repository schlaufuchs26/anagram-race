Welcome to Anagram Race

Test your vocabulary skills! You have 60 seconds per round to find as many valid English words as possible from scrambled letters.

How to Play:

  • Each round gives you 7-8 scrambled letters
  • Form words using any of the letters (minimum 3 letters)
  • Each word is worth points equal to its length
  • No duplicate words in the same round
  • You have 15 challenging rounds
  • Green = valid word, Red = invalid
